Double-Click slider to reset value to clear settings. Loads a random image from Unsplash when the page loads unless a local file has been previously chosen.

I was sick of having to open up photoshop or photopea just to quickly blur a still image for a stream background. This site is designed to make that process easier. Just take a snapshot, choose the file, and BAM! The image will be blurred and darkened to roughly my taste. Press "Hide Menu" to hide the menu. Once hidden, click anywhere on the canvas to show the menu again. Please note that due to vMix's interactions with browser storage, the settings will not be retained when re-loading vmix or refreshing the browser input. Sozzles
